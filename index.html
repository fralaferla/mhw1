This is a picture taken in Ilha do Pico. Azores are volcanic islands in the middle of the Atlantic. Pico is the tallest mountain -former active volcano- in Portugal.

This is just a sample of their green landscape. Azores are one of the most rainy places on the planet, forcing me to wear my waterproof gear most of the time.

Horta, even though it is home to only 15000 people approximately, is quite young. You can hang out at their best pub, Peter's, and have a sip of the best gin ever made.
